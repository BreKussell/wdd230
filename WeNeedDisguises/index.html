What is Cosplay?

Simply put, Cosplay is dressing up in a costume with added role-playing. Cosplay is compounded from the words 'costume' and 'role-play,' where the person dressing up represents a specific character, canon or fan-made, style, or even an idea. In other words, the person is dressing up as the character and acts as if they were the character (but that second part isn't required). Cosplay isn't about the size/quality/price of the costume. It's about having fun while doing it.
